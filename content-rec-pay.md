**Managed Payroll**
Everything HMRC requires, handled completely
We take full responsibility for your payroll from data collection to RTI submission. You review the figures and approve — we do everything else.

Services:
01. PAYE & Income Tax — Tax calculated per employee using the correct code, personal allowance, basic, higher, and additional rate bands applied accurately.
02. National Insurance — Employer and employee NI across all categories (A, B, C, H, J, M, V, X) reported correctly to HMRC via RTI every period.
03. RTI Submission — Full Payment Submissions (FPS) and Employer Payment Summaries (EPS) filed on time. All in-year amendments handled.
04. Statutory Payments — SMP, SPP, SAP, ShPP, and SSP processed at the correct statutory rates every pay period without exception.
05. Student Loan Deductions — Plans 1, 2, and 4 applied at the correct threshold and rate. HMRC start and stop notices actioned immediately.
06. Pension Auto-Enrolment — Employee and employer contributions calculated within the qualifying earnings band. Data provided to your pension provider each period.
07. P45s, P60s & Payslips — P45 on the day of leaving. P60 at year-end. Digital payslips distributed to every employee every pay period.
08. Year-End & Compliance — Final EPS, year-end reconciliation, and P11D support. We track every HMRC rate change so your payroll stays compliant.

**HMRC Compliance**
Late payroll costs more than outsourcing it
HMRC issues automatic penalties for late or incorrect RTI filings. They apply per month and stack. A single penalty can exceed what we charge for a full year.

Automatic RTI Penalty Schedule:
- 1–9 employees: £100 / month
- 10–49 employees: £200 / month
- 50–249 employees: £300 / month
- 250+ employees: £400 / month

Income Tax (PAYE) Regulations 2003 — HMRC in-year filing penalties.
